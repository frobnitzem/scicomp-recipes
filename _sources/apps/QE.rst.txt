Running Quantum Espresso on Summit
##################################

An initial GPU-accelerated version of Quantum Espresso was developed by 
Massimiliano Fatica, Everett Phillips, Josh Romero, and Filippo Spiga around 2018.
here (https://github.com/fspiga/qe-gpu).
This is the presentation: http://indico.ictp.it/event/8004/session/107/contribution/220/material/slides/0.pdf

Recently, the main QE development effort has also released a Quantum Espresso
here (https://gitlab.com/QEF/q-e-gpu).
